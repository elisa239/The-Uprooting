The Uprooting is a multimedia documentary project that explores the refugee crisis through the life-stories of its protagonists. It aims to go beyond the daily headlines and forefront the voices of refugees themselves in an in-depth examination of the traumas of war, the challenges of exile and the contours of an evolving diaspora.
